Welcome!

I'm a Schmidt Fellow at the University of Chicago. I think about the detection, analysis, and interpretation of gravitational waves as tools with which to test physics, understand stars, and study the distant Universe.

Before Chicago, I worked as a Research Fellow at the Flatiron Institute's Center for Computational Astrophysics. I attended Carleton College for my undergraduate education, after which I obtained an M.Phil. in Astronomy at the University of Cambridge and my Ph.D. in Physics at Caltech.
